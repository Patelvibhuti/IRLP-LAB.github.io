About IRLP Lab

The Information Retrieval and Language Processing Lab (IRLP Lab) was established in the year 2010 with the aim of doing research in the area of information retrieval (IR) wherein we deal with retrieval of information from unstructured documents and natural language processing (NLP) wherein we deal with processing of Indic languages.

The objective of IRLP Lab is to do research as well as deliver prototypes based on this research which can be used by the society.

Research Focus

Cross Lingual Information Retrieval, Financial Text Mining, Legal AI

Funding Achievement

Received total funds worth Rs. 3.3 crore for ongoing and completed projects

Research Areas

Our research spans across various domains of Information Retrieval and Natural Language Processing

Cross Lingual Information Retrieval

Developing systems for retrieving information across different languages

Financial Text Mining

Analyzing and extracting insights from financial documents and data

Legal AI

Applying AI techniques to legal document analysis and processing

Recommendation Systems

Building intelligent systems for personalized content recommendations

Machine Translation

Developing systems for automated translation between languages

Social Media Analytics

Analyzing social media data for insights and patterns
